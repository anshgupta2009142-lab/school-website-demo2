About Our School

Delhi Public School is committed to providing world-class education that develops academically excellent, ethically grounded, and socially responsible citizens.

With over 35 years of excellence, we have established ourselves as a leading educational institution in Delhi. Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-centric approach ensure that every child reaches their full potential.

Our Mission: To impart quality education that combines academic rigor with holistic development.

Our Vision: To create a learning environment where every student can pursue excellence and develop into responsible global citizens.
